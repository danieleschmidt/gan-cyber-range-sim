# GAN Cyber Range Simulator - Project Roadmap

## Vision
Create the world's first open-source Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) cyber range that enables realistic, AI-powered red team vs blue team simulations for cybersecurity training and research.

## Current Version: 0.1.0 (Alpha)

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **LLM Reliability**: Implement fallback strategies and human oversight
- **Security Isolation**: Regular penetration testing of isolation mechanisms
- **Performance Scaling**: Horizontal scaling architecture from day one
- **Tool Integration**: Abstraction layers for easy tool replacement

### Business Risks
- **Funding**: Diversified funding from grants, donations, and commercial services
- **Competition**: Focus on open source advantage and community building
- **Regulation**: Proactive compliance with cybersecurity regulations
- **Misuse**: Strong ethical guidelines and usage monitoring
